Inspired by the play Mumbai Bookies, based on Shantanu Naidu’s real life initiative, this project captures the quiet act of reading together in a bustling city. Every Sunday, strangers gather in silence to find peace and connection through books. My three posters reflect moods of discovery, pause, and calm without showing books directly. Through subtle imagery, typography, and color, I aimed to evoke the serenity, shared stillness, and poetic rhythm of Mumbai's collective moments of reflection.

A luxurious and cinematic poster celebrating the essence of fine wine. Minimal yet rich aesthetics communicate exclusivity.

A quiet moment of warmth. This minimal design mirrors the comfort of a clean and inviting meal.

Bursting with energy and flavor, this dynamic ad uses texture, color, and motion to evoke instant craving and joy.

Sleek and futuristic — a modern campaign design showcasing innovation, elegance, and effortless functionality.

Where style meets substance — earthy tones and tactile visuals express craftsmanship and authenticity.
